# 🌌 QuantumLedger

**Next-Generation Desktop CRM, Billing, and Inventory Management System**

QuantumLedger is a powerful, offline-first desktop application designed to streamline retail operations, client management, and financial tracking. Built for speed and reliability, it eliminates the need for expensive cloud subscriptions while offering enterprise-grade features like automated WhatsApp invoicing, professional PDF reporting, and secure data encryption.

-----

## 🎯 Who is QuantumLedger For?

This software was purpose-built to solve the exact pain points of local commerce:

  * **Small Business Owners:** Say goodbye to messy, time-consuming pen-and-paper ledgers. QuantumLedger digitizes your entire workflow, saving you hours of manual calculation at the end of the day and completely eliminating human error.
  * **Local Vendors & Retailers:** Perfect for brick-and-mortar shops that need lightning-fast billing while a customer is standing at the counter waiting.
  * **Bootstrapped Startups:** Look like a massive enterprise with beautifully branded PDF invoices and automated WhatsApp receipts, all without paying exorbitant monthly fees for cloud-based SaaS products.

-----

## ⚡ What Makes QuantumLedger Powerful? (The Competitive Edge)

Most modern CRMs trap business owners in endless monthly SaaS subscriptions and hold their data hostage in the cloud. QuantumLedger breaks that mold:

  * **100% Data Sovereignty (Offline-First):** Your business data, customer lists, and financial records live on *your* machine, not on a third-party server. Complete privacy and zero reliance on an internet connection for core operations.
  * **Zero-Click WhatsApp Integration:** Unlike web-based CRMs that require expensive API keys to send messages, QuantumLedger natively hooks into WhatsApp Web to send instant digital invoices to customers for free.
  * **No Recurring Fees:** A standalone executable that runs flawlessly without a monthly subscription.
  * **Military-Grade Local Security:** Built-in PIN authentication and recovery systems ensure that unauthorized personnel cannot access your financial ledgers.
  * **Frictionless Dues Management:** Automatically calculates and carries forward previous customer balances into new invoices, making credit management effortless.

## 🛠️ Core Features

### 📦 Smart Inventory Management

  * **Dynamic Stock Tracking:** Add, edit, and monitor stock levels in real-time.
  * **Custom Units:** Sell items by the pack, bag, litre, box, kg, or create your own custom measurement units on the fly.
  * **Low Stock Alerts:** Visual indicators flag items that are running low (≤ 5 units) directly on the billing screen and in exported reports.

### 👥 Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

  * **Detailed Profiles:** Store comprehensive customer details including names, phone numbers, addresses, and guardian details.
  * **Credit/Ledger Tracking:** Maintain a running tally of outstanding balances for every customer.

### 🧾 Advanced Billing Engine

  * **Lightning-Fast Cart:** Search and add items to a customer's bill in seconds.
  * **Financial Adjustments:** Instantly apply percentage-based discounts and GST to the subtotal.
  * **Split Payments:** Record partial payments and automatically add the remaining balance to the customer's permanent due ledger.
  * **Private Remarks:** Add internal notes to local invoice copies that remain hidden from the customer's WhatsApp receipt.

### 📊 Analytics & Reporting

  * **Historical Invoice Vault:** Search, view, and reprint any past invoice by ID or customer name.
  * **Professional PDF Exports:** Generate beautiful, branded A4-sized PDF invoices containing your Shop Name, GSTIN, and contact details.
  * **Business Dashboards:** Export 1-click PDF reports for Total Sales, Customer Outstanding Balances, and Low Stock inventory.

## 🔄 The QuantumLedger Workflow

1.  **Authenticate:** Launch the app and bypass the security gate using your custom PIN.
2.  **Stock & Setup:** Input your inventory and define your business details (GSTIN, Shop Name) in the Settings panel.
3.  **Create a Bill:** Select an existing customer or quick-add a new one. The system instantly retrieves their previous dues.
4.  **Checkout:** Add items, apply taxes/discounts, and log the received payment.
5.  **Dispatch:** Click "Save & Send WhatsApp" to log the transaction locally and instantly route a formatted digital receipt to the customer's phone.

## 💻 Technical Stack & Libraries

QuantumLedger is engineered using robust, lightweight technologies to ensure maximum compatibility and low system overhead:

  * **Language:** Python 3.x
  * **GUI Framework:** `tkinter` & `ttk` (Native, fast, and responsive user interface)
  * **Database:** `sqlite3` (Serverless, self-contained SQL database engine)
  * **PDF Generation:** `fpdf` (For generating highly customized, professional analytics reports and invoices)
  * **Automation:** `pywhatkit` (For seamless, browser-based WhatsApp message queuing and dispatch)
  * **Core Utilities:** `os`, `threading`, `datetime` (For asynchronous background tasks and local file management)
